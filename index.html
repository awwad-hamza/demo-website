ENGINEERING CHALLENGES

The parts that had to be tuned, not just shown.

SLAM

SLAM Drift

Map alignment can degrade during longer runs.

Tuned: loop closure checks, motion speed, sensor placement.
Nav2

Costmap Tuning

Too much inflation blocks paths; too little creates risky plans.

Tuned: inflation radius, obstacle persistence, footprint.
Sensors

Sensor Noise

Real readings introduce false edges and unstable obstacle points.

Tested: filtering, update rates, mounting height.
Localization

Pose Recovery

Coverage depends on stable pose estimates while the robot builds and follows its map.

Tuned: AMCL/pose parameters, startup routine, motion speed.
Deployment

Sim-to-Real Gaps

Simulation is clean; floors, wheels, and lighting are not.

Tested: speed limits, acceleration, obstacle margins.
